HACKATHON

The 24-Hour Hackathon is a state-level competition designed to bring together bright minds from across educational institutions to collaborate, innovate, and solve real-world challenges. Participants will work intensively in teams, turning their ideas into practical solutions under time constraints. With mentorship, milestone reviews, and final evaluations by expert judges, the hackathon provides a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, creativity, teamwork, and entrepreneurial thinking. Attractive prizes await the most impactful and scalable solutions, making this event a unique opportunity to learn, network, and compete.
