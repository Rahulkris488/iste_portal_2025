What to Expect

The Project Expo is a platform for students to showcase innovative ideas, technical knowledge, and problem-solving skills, aiming to enhance presentation skills and foster innovation among peers, faculty, and industry experts.

Event Guidelines

Eligibility & Participation

  • Students from any branch or discipline are eligible.
  • Participants must carry a valid college ID card.
  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.

Exhibition Setup

  • Each team will be provided a stall/table for their demonstration.
  • One power socket (230V, 50Hz AC) will be available per team.
  • Flammable fluids or hazardous materials are strictly prohibited.

Judging & Evaluation

  • Projects are evaluated on innovation, working demonstration, and presentation quality.
  • Prizes will be awarded to the best projects.
  • The decision of the judges will be final.
