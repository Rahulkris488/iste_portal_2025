Innovation Showcase

A platform for students to showcase their innovative ideas, technical knowledge, and problem-solving skills through live demonstrations and working models. The event encourages creativity and interdisciplinary collaboration by welcoming participants from all branches of study.

// EXHIBITION PROTOCOL

  • Teams: 2 to 4 members from any discipline.
  • Setup: Dedicated stall with one power socket. Internet on request.
  • Requirements: A working model and summary poster are mandatory.
  • Judging: Based on innovation, demonstration, and applicability.
  • Conduct: No hazardous materials. Professionalism is expected.
  • Eligibility: Participants must carry a valid college ID card.
