What to Expect

The CTF competition fosters analytical thinking and teamwork in a controlled, ethical environment. The team that demonstrates both speed and accuracy in solving challenges will emerge as the winner.

Event Guidelines

General Rules

  • Each team must consist of exactly two members.
  • All teams must register before the start of the competition.
  • Sharing flags or solutions between teams is strictly prohibited.
  • For any queries, seek help only from designated volunteers.

Technical Rules

  • Participants will use a dedicated computer in a standardized lab environment.
  • Attacks on the CTF infrastructure (e.g., DoS, brute-force) are forbidden.
  • Participants may only use the standard tools provided in the lab.
  • Use of AI tools, automated scanners, or external scripts is strictly prohibited.

Scoring & Submissions

  • Points are awarded for each successful flag submission.
  • In case of a tie, the team that reached the final score first will win.
  • Flags must be submitted only via the official CTF platform.
