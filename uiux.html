UI/UX Design Challenge

The UI/UX Design Challenge is a one-day competition that tests participants’ creativity, problem-solving, and design skills under real-time pressure. Participants will be given a surprise theme and will work to create user-friendly, visually appealing, and original digital designs. The event encourages innovation, clarity in design thinking, and effective presentation skills, while simulating the challenges faced in real-world product development.

The Challenge Briefing

You'll be given a surprise theme on the spot. From there, you'll have 2.5 hours to research, design, and present a user-friendly and visually appealing digital solution.

Individual or Duo

Compete solo or team up with a partner to tackle the challenge.

On-the-Spot Theme

No advance prep. Think fast and design smart based on the given topic.

Figma Powered

Systems with Figma pre-installed will be provided for everyone.

Design Process & Rules

Participation & Design

  • Open to individuals or teams of a maximum of 2 members.
  • Participants must be registered before the event begins.
  • Work must begin only after the theme is revealed.
  • Systems with Figma (free version) pre-installed will be provided.
  • Internet is for assets & references only; no pre-made UI kits or templates.
  • Deliverables: Wireframes, 3-5 core UI screens, and a 100-150 word design rationale.
  • Submissions must be on time via Figma link, PDF, or PNGs. Late entries are not evaluated.

Conduct & Discipline

  • Participants must report to the venue on time.
  • Respect fellow participants, mentors, and judges.
  • Cheating or misbehavior will lead to disqualification.
  • Organizers may make adjustments during the event if required.

Fair Play & Judging

  • Plagiarism or using pre-built templates leads to immediate disqualification.
  • AI tools for ideation or design generation are strictly prohibited.
  • Offensive or inappropriate designs will not be accepted.
  • The decisions of the judging panel are final and binding.

Judging Criteria (100 Pts)

  • Creativity & Originality: 25 pts
  • User Experience (Flow, Usability): 25 pts
  • Visual Appeal & Consistency: 20 pts
  • Relevance to Theme / Problem Solving: 20 pts
  • Presentation & Clarity of Thought: 10 pts
