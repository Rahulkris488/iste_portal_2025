About ISTE

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), established in 1941, is a leading national non-profit organization dedicated to advancing engineering and technology education. Registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860, ISTE has built a robust network with over 1,35,525 technical teachers, 500,000 student members, and numerous institutional and faculty chapters across India.
