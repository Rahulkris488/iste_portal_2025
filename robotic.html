// ROBOTIC ARENAS

Robo Kick-Off

A dynamic soccer-style game where bots must maneuver a ball and defend a goal, testing technical skill and teamwork.

An autonomous challenge where bots must follow a predefined track using sensors, testing automation and precision.

Two bots compete to push each other out of a circular ring in a test of design, balance, power, and strategy.

An autonomous navigation challenge where bots must solve a maze filled with obstacles, testing problem-solving skills.
