[ Project Description ]

AutoCAD is one of the most widely used computer-aided design (CAD) software for creating precise 2D and 3D designs. This competition provides a platform for students to showcase their drafting skills and creativity. Participants will be challenged to replicate a given PDF drawing, testing their accuracy, speed, and attention to detail.

TIME: 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM
VENUE: IT Block
HOSTED BY: Mechanical Engineering Department

[ Rules & Regulations ]

  • Maximum 1 participant per team (individual participation only).
  • A PDF drawing will be provided at the time of the competition.
  • Use of personal electronic accessories (mobile phones, USB drives, internet, etc.) is strictly prohibited.
  • Participants must complete their design within the specified time limit.

[ Judging Criteria ]

  • Accuracy and completeness of the drawing.
  • Time taken to finish the task (optimum time).
