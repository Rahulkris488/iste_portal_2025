The Premise

The Tech Debate is a platform for students to showcase critical thinking and communication skills on emerging tech topics. Participants will be evaluated on logical arguments, fluency, and prompt responses in a structured, competitive setting.

Rules of Engagement

Structure & Timing

  • Selection: Six teams will be shortlisted for the final round after prelims.
  • Topic: The debate topic will be provided on the spot.
  • Total Time: Each team has a total of 7 minutes.
  • Speaker Time: Individual speakers must not exceed 2 minutes per argument.

Conduct & Integrity

  • No Aids: Electronic gadgets or reading from paper are strictly prohibited.
  • Professionalism: Use of offensive language or gestures is not allowed.
  • Fair Play: Any malpractice will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Finality: The decision of the judges and organizers is final.

Judging Matrix

Relevance

Clarity and logical consistency of arguments.

Fluency

Confidence and clarity of speech.

Involvement

Active participation and engagement.

Promptness

Quick thinking in rebuttals and responses.
