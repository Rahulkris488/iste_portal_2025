Lakshman Rekha

THE LINE FOLLOWER CHALLENGE

Date: 11th & 12th Nov, 2025 | Time: 9 AM (11th) to 3 PM (12th)

The Lakshman Rekha competition challenges participants to design and build fully autonomous robots that can follow a predefined track. The event tests automation, embedded systems, and programming skills, encouraging students to innovate in the fundamentals of robotics and AI.

Sacred Protocols

Robot Specifications

  • Maximum dimensions: 20cm x 20cm x 20cm.
  • Maximum weight: 3 kg.
  • Must function under varying lighting conditions.
  • All systems (battery, sensors, control) must be onboard.
  • Power supply must not exceed 12V; no external power is allowed.

Rules of the Arena

  • The arena map will be disclosed on the day of the event.
  • The arena track width will be approximately 3 cm.
  • Once the robot crosses the start line, it must remain fully autonomous.
  • Manual touches to the bot will incur penalties. Score is calculated as completion time minus penalties.
  • The decisions of the judges and coordinators are final and binding.
