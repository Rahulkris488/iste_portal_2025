The Technical Quiz is an engaging knowledge-based competition designed to test participants’ awareness of current and emerging technologies. Conducted through the Unstop platform , the quiz challenges students with multiple-choice questions across diverse domains. With a real-time leaderboard and timed rounds, the event encourages quick thinking and accuracy, creating a competitive environment where participants can showcase their technical knowledge and problem-solving skills.

// RULES OF ENGAGEMENT //

  • Team Composition: Up to 3 members. Lone participants will be grouped on-site.
  • Environment: This is a closed-book competition. No internet or external resources are permitted.
  • Device Policy: All mobile phones must be switched off and placed face-down on the desk.
  • Collaboration: Discussion is allowed only within your team, at a low volume.
  • Authority: The Quiz Master's decision is final and binding during the event.
  • Tie-Breakers: Prelims will use star-marked questions, followed by a sudden-death round if needed. Finals will use a sudden-death tie-breaker round.
