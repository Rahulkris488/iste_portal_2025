ROBO RACE

Date: 12th Nov, 2025 | Time: 09:00 AM - 03:30 PM

The Robo Race is a high-energy robotics competition where participants design and operate robots to navigate a track filled with obstacles such as humps, slopes, and elevations. The goal is to complete the course in the shortest possible time without errors. The event emphasizes speed, control, design efficiency, and endurance of the bots, while challenging students’ technical and problem-solving skills.

// RACE PROTOCOLS //

Bot Specifications

  • Maximum Dimensions: 30 cm × 25 cm × 25 cm (l × b × h).
  • Maximum Weight: 3 kg (±5% tolerance).
  • Power: On-board or off-board supply permitted.
  • Both wired and wireless bots are allowed.
  • Coordinators not responsible for electromagnetic interference.

Rules & Regulations

  • Only students from the same college can form a team.
  • One individual cannot be part of multiple teams.
  • No ready-made market kits allowed; bots must be self-constructed.
  • Robots must follow specifications (violations will lead to disqualification).
  • No practice runs on the main track.
  • The winner is the team completing the track in the least time.
  • The structure of the robot cannot be modified during the competition.
  • If a robot falls off or is immobile, it will be reset to the last checkpoint with a penalty.
  • Pulling the bot using wires will lead to disqualification.
  • The robot must not be disassembled until results are declared.
  • The time measured by organizers is final.
